Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can GoAbroad help me study overseas?

We guide you through every step — from selecting a university and applying for admission to securing your visa and travel assistance.

2. Which countries do you provide assistance for?

We help students apply to universities in the USA, UK, Canada, and Australia.

3. Is there any consultation fee?

Our first counselling session is completely free! Further services depend on the program and destination country.

4. Can you help with scholarships?

Yes! We help students find and apply for merit-based and need-based scholarships worldwide.

5. How can I contact GoAbroad?

The exams required depend on your destination country and course. Most universities ask for IELTS or TOEFL to prove English language proficiency. Some postgraduate programs may also require GRE or GMAT scores. Always check the official university website for exact admission requirements.
